Tate McRae

Biography

Tate McRae is a Canadian singer, songwriter, and dancer, born on July 1, 2003. She first gained attention as a finalist on So You Think You Can Dance before launching her music career on YouTube. She signed with RCA Records in 2019 and rose to global fame with her 2020 hit “you broke me first”. Her singles like “greedy” and albums including Think Later have made her one of the most popular young pop artists today, known for her emotional lyrics and modern pop sound.
